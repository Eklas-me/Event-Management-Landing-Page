What Is Tour Intell Platform

About

Tour intell is designed to give tour managers, road managers a streamlined, centralized way to manage all the moving parts associated with running a professional music tour. Tour Intell lets managers enter all info related to shows, rehearsals, travel, appearances, and bus calls to help coordination, Users are assigned different access roles within the system to control privacy yet allow for flexibility keeping the crew well informed every step of the way.
